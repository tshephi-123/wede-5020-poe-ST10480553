Guardian of Life provides compassion, confidential, and free support to expectant mothers, young families, and individuals navigating uplanned pregnancies.

Our Purpose

We believe that every human life is valuable and deserve to be protected and nurtured. Through education, material aid, and professional councelling, we empower individuals by providing real alternatives and comprehensive care, ensuring that no mother has to face challanges unsupported.

How We Can Help You

Free Community Counselling

Speak confidentially with our compassionate counsellors, who offer emotional and practical guidance tailored to your unique situation.

Prenatal & Parenting Education

Join our structured educational classes designed to help you with the essential knowledge needed for a healthy pregnacy and infant care.

Material Assistance

Access free care package containing baby clothing, blankets, diapers, formula and other essenial items to support your child's growth.
